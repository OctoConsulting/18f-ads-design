Rope in the Knowledge with Lasso

Lasso is an openFDA driven solution that provides you with a safety assessment of your drug of interest. Pulling data from the adverse event and recall data from openFDA’s drug dataset, Lasso provides a holistic and filterable view of a drug’s adverse events and recalls.

  • Lasso Highlights
  • Top Searches

Learn

Adverse Event Report are submitted to the FDA to report any undesirable experience associated with the use of a medical product in a patient.

Discover

Recalls are actions taken by a firm to remove a product from the market. Recalls may be conducted on a firm's own initiative, by FDA request, or by FDA order under statutory authority.

Explore

Lasso allows you to filter the data to narrow down a drug’s data to a desired subset.
